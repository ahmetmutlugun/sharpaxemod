A mod made by sharpaxe (obviously).

Currently adds 2 axes, 2 guns, 4 swords and 2 special projectiles.

I finally fixed my tModLoader so I can develop on my Mac, should make things a lot faster. (December 2019)

Balanced weapon damage

Many bug fixes
